# Warpkeep Code of Conduct

Warpkeep welcomes players and contributors who want to help build a thoughtful,
open strategy world. Project spaces should remain useful, safe, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful when discussing people, ideas, and unfinished work.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good faith while asking for clarity.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and the project's security boundaries.
- Make room for newcomers and for disagreement without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention, sustained
disruption, impersonation, and publishing another person's private information
are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies to the Warpkeep repository, issue tracker, review threads,
and project-managed community spaces. The maintainer may edit or remove content,
close discussions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
needed to protect the community.

Do not post sensitive evidence publicly. To report a conduct concern privately,
use the private-contact request process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), identify
it as a conduct concern, and include no personal or sensitive details until a
private channel is established. Reports will be handled as privately and fairly
as practical for a one-person project.
