# Warpkeep Trademarks and Official Identity

The open-source and Creative Commons licenses do not grant trademark rights or permission to imply official status.

`Warpkeep`, its logos, the official domain `warpkeep.com`, official social accounts, and the canonical hosted realm identify the official Warpkeep project. This document does not claim that any mark is registered.

Forks, mods, and community realms may accurately say that they are based on, compatible with, or derived from Warpkeep. They should use a distinct project name and branding for their own deployment and may not imply sponsorship, endorsement, or official status.

Nominative and descriptive references to Warpkeep are allowed when they truthfully identify compatibility, origin, or discussion. Do not use the official name, logos, domain, accounts, or deployment identity in a way that could confuse players about who operates a service.

This policy is intended to preserve clear attribution and official-project identity while remaining friendly to forks, mods, community realms, and independent implementations. It does not create legal enforcement powers beyond rights that actually exist.
